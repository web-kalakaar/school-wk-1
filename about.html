About Us

A school built on trust, tradition and forward thinking

Sant Kirpal Senior Secondary School was founded in 1998 in Bharatpur with a simple belief: every child deserves an education that is both academically rigorous and genuinely caring.

What began as a small neighbourhood school with a handful of classrooms has grown into one of the region's most respected RBSE-affiliated institutions, serving over 2,400 students from Nursery to Class XII. Our growth has never come at the cost of our founding values — small class sizes, attentive teachers, and a campus where every student is known by name.

Today, our campus combines smart classrooms, well-equipped science and computer labs, a library, and dedicated sports facilities — all designed to support the way children actually learn: through curiosity, practice, and encouragement.

What Drives Us

Our vision, mission & values

The principles that guide every decision we make, from the classroom to the boardroom.

Our Vision

To be the region's most trusted institution for academic excellence, character building and future-ready learning, recognised for producing confident, capable citizens.

Our Mission

To deliver quality, values-based education that empowers every student to reach their full potential — academically, socially and emotionally — in a safe and nurturing environment.

Our Core Values

Integrity, respect, curiosity and discipline. We believe strong character is built alongside strong academics — one does not come at the expense of the other.
