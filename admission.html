Before You Apply

Eligibility & required documents

Please review the age criteria and documentation needed to complete your child's admission.

Age Eligibility Criteria

  • Nursery: Child must be 3+ years old as of 31st March of the admission year.
  • Class I: Child must be 5+ years old as of 31st March of the admission year.
  • Class VI–VIII: Age-appropriate as per previous class completed, verified against birth certificate.
  • Class IX–X: Must have passed the previous class from a recognised RBSE/CBSE school.
  • Class XI: Must have passed Class X with minimum qualifying marks in the chosen stream's core subjects.

Documents Required

  • Original birth certificate and one photocopy
  • Previous school's report card / marksheet
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from the previous school, if applicable
  • Address proof (Aadhaar card, utility bill, or ration card)
  • 4 recent passport-size photographs of the student
  • Caste/category certificate, if applying under a reserved category
