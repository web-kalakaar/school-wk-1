About Our School

A legacy of learning, built for tomorrow

Since 1998, Radhe Krishna Senior Secondary School has combined a disciplined RBSE curriculum with modern teaching methods, giving every student the foundation to excel academically and grow into a well-rounded individual.

Our Mission

To deliver quality, values-based education that empowers every student to reach their full potential.

Our Vision

To be the region's most trusted school for academic excellence and character building.
