Curriculum & Programs

Academics at Sant Kirpal

A structured, RBSE-aligned curriculum designed around the developmental needs of every age group — from first steps to board success.

Home / Academics
Curriculum Information

How learning progresses at our school

Our academic program is organised into four wings, each building on the last with age-appropriate teaching methods and clear learning outcomes.

RBSE Aligned

Every wing follows the official RBSE syllabus, ensuring students stay fully prepared for board assessments.

Activity-Based Learning

Concepts are reinforced through projects, labs and group activities, not just textbooks and lectures.

Continuous Assessment

Regular unit tests and progress tracking help identify and support each student well before exams.

Stream Choice at +2

Science, Commerce and Arts streams at senior secondary level, guided by career counselling sessions.

Class I – V
Primary Wing

Building strong foundations, one step at a time

The Primary Wing focuses on nurturing curiosity and building strong fundamentals in literacy and numeracy through a warm, activity-rich environment where young learners feel safe to explore.

Main Focus

Foundational literacy, numeracy, motor skills and social-emotional development through play-based and activity-led teaching.

Key Features

  • Play-based, activity-led curriculum for ages 5–10
  • Low teacher-to-student ratio for individual attention
  • Interactive smart boards in every classroom
  • Dedicated phonics and reading development program
Additional Features
  • Weekly art, craft and music sessions to build creativity
  • Storytelling and puppet-show based value education
  • Monthly parent-teacher interaction for early feedback
  • On-campus nurse and first-aid facility for younger children
  • Supervised indoor and outdoor play periods daily

Class VI – VIII
Middle Wing

Where subject specialisation begins

The Middle Wing introduces students to distinct subjects taught by specialist teachers, with hands-on lab work and project-based learning that builds analytical thinking ahead of the board years.

Main Focus

Subject-wise depth, scientific reasoning, project-based assessment and early introduction to computer literacy.

Key Features

  • Dedicated science and computer labs with hands-on sessions
  • Subject-specialist teachers for every core subject
  • Regular project work and classroom presentations
  • Introduction to structured exam patterns and revision habits
Additional Features
  • Inter-house quiz, debate and science exhibition events
  • Career awareness sessions to help students discover interests
  • Remedial classes for students needing extra academic support
  • Introduction to basic coding and digital literacy tools
  • House-based sports and cultural competitions

Class IX – X
Secondary Wing

Focused, disciplined board exam preparation

The Secondary Wing centres entirely around RBSE board readiness — structured revision cycles, frequent assessments and one-on-one doubt-clearing to make sure every student walks into the exam hall prepared.

Main Focus

RBSE board exam readiness, strong fundamentals across core subjects, and structured career counselling for stream selection.

Key Features

  • Structured RBSE board exam preparation plan
  • Weekly mock tests with detailed performance analysis
  • One-on-one doubt-clearing sessions with subject teachers
  • Career counselling to guide stream selection for Class XI
Additional Features
  • Previous years' question paper practice and analysis
  • Special revision camps in the weeks before board exams
  • Parent-teacher meetings focused on individual improvement plans
  • Guest lectures from alumni and industry professionals
  • Stress-management and exam-readiness workshops

Subjects Taught

  • English
  • Hindi
  • Mathematics

  • Social Science

Additional Subjects & Activities
  • Physical Education
  • Art Education
  • Career Guidance
Class XI – XII
Senior Secondary Wing

Preparing confident students for competitive futures

The Senior Secondary Wing offers Science, Commerce and Arts streams taught by experienced subject faculty, with a curriculum designed to prepare students for both board exams and competitive entrance tests.

Main Focus

Deep subject mastery within chosen streams, board and competitive exam preparation, and readiness for higher education.

Key Features

  • Choice of Science, Commerce and Arts streams
  • Subject-matter experts with years of board-teaching experience
  • Regular practical sessions in physics, chemistry and biology labs
  • Foundation support for competitive exams alongside board prep
Additional Features
  • Dedicated doubt-clearing hours outside regular class time
  • Full-length mock board and competitive exam test series
  • College and career counselling with stream-specific guidance
  • Workshops on time management and exam strategy
  • Access to well-stocked reference library and past papers
