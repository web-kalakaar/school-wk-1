Life On Campus

Campus Life at Radhe Krishna

Scroll through our infrastructure, classrooms, labs, sports facilities, activities, events and educational trips — a complete picture of everyday life on campus.

Home / Campus Life
01 / 07

World-class infrastructure built for learning

Our campus is designed to feel spacious, safe and inspiring — from the main academic block to quiet reading corners, every space serves a purpose in a student's day.

  • Spread across a secure, well-planned campus
  • Central library with thousands of titles and reading zones
  • Multipurpose auditorium for assemblies and events
  • CCTV-monitored corridors and entry points throughout
02 / 07

Modern classrooms that keep students engaged

Every classroom is equipped with interactive smart boards and flexible seating, helping teachers bring lessons to life for every learning style.

  • Interactive smart boards in every classroom
  • Bright, well-ventilated spaces with natural light
  • Flexible seating for group and activity-based learning
  • Dedicated activity rooms for hands-on projects
03 / 07

Fully equipped labs for hands-on science

Theory comes alive in our physics, chemistry, biology and computer labs — spaces where students test ideas, run experiments and build real technical skills.

  • Dedicated Physics, Chemistry and Biology laboratories
  • Computer lab with updated systems and internet access
  • Supervised, safety-first practical sessions every week
  • Equipment aligned with the RBSE practical curriculum
04 / 07

Sports facilities that build fitness and teamwork

From daily physical education to inter-house tournaments, our sports program helps students build discipline, resilience and lifelong healthy habits.

  • Basketball, volleyball and cricket facilities on campus
  • Dedicated athletics track for annual sports day
  • Indoor games room for table tennis and chess
  • Daily yoga and fitness sessions for all age groups
05 / 07

Activities and clubs beyond the classroom

We believe every student has a talent waiting to be discovered — our clubs give them the space and mentorship to explore art, music, dance and public speaking.

  • Art & craft club with regular exhibitions
  • Music and instrument practice sessions
  • Dance and performance training for school events
  • Debate and elocution society for public speaking skills
06 / 07

Events and celebrations throughout the year

From the Annual Day to festival celebrations, our school calendar is full of moments that bring students, teachers and parents together.

  • Annual Day with performances from every wing
  • Independence Day and Republic Day functions
  • Annual sports day and inter-house competitions
  • Festival celebrations promoting cultural awareness
07 / 07

Educational trips that extend learning beyond books

Carefully planned excursions connect classroom learning to the real world, from heritage sites to science exhibitions and industrial visits.

  • Historical and heritage site excursions
  • Science city and exhibition visits
  • Adventure and nature camps for team-building
  • Industrial and career-oriented field visits
